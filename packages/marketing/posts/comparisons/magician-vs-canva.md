# Magician vs Canva: Which is Better?

AI design tools inside Figma

**Pros:** Works inside Figma, Great for ideation
**Cons:** Requires Figma subscription, Limited free credits
**Pricing:** Free / From $9/mo

## Canva

Design anything with AI magic

**Pros:** Extremely easy to use, Huge template library
**Cons:** Less precise than pro tools, Can feel limiting for experts
**Pricing:** Free / From $13/mo
