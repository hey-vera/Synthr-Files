# Runway ML Review: AI video generation and editing

Runway ML is the leading AI video editing platform. Gen-3 generates high-quality video from text and images, while Motion Brush lets you animate specific parts of an image. It's essential for video creators, motion designers, and anyone exploring AI-generated video content. The quality improvements with each generation are remarkable.
