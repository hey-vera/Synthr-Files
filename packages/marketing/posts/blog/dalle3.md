# DALL-E 3 Review: OpenAI's latest image generation

## What is DALL-E 3?

DALL-E 3 is OpenAI's most advanced image generation model, available through ChatGPT Plus and the API. It excels at understanding complex prompts with multiple objects and relationships. The integration with ChatGPT makes it incredibly accessible for anyone already using the platform. The quality rivals Midjourney with better prompt adherence.
