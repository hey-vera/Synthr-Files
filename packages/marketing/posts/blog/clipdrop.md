# Clipdrop Review: Stable Diffusion tools for creators

## What is Clipdrop?

Clipdrop by Stability AI provides a suite of AI image editing tools including relighting, upscaling, background removal, and generative fill. The API-first approach makes it perfect for developers and designers building AI-powered products. The quality is excellent and the pricing is more competitive than many alternatives.
