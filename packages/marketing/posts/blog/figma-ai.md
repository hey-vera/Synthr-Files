# Figma AI Review: Native AI features in Figma

## What is Figma AI?

Figma AI brings generative features directly into the design tool you already use. Auto-layout suggestions, AI-generated images, and first-draft designs from prompts are now built-in. As a native feature, it understands your design system and components better than any plugin. Essential for teams already in the Figma ecosystem.

## Key Features

- **First drafts**: First drafts
- **AI images**: AI images
- **Auto layout**: Auto layout
- **Asset search**: Asset search

## Pros

- Native integration
- Understands design systems
- No context switching

## Cons

- Requires Figma subscription
- Limited to Figma workflows
- Still improving

## Who Should Use It?

Figma AI is ideal for ui/ux design who need first drafts without the steep learning curve.
