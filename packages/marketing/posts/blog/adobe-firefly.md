# Adobe Firefly Review: Safe AI for commercial design

## What is Adobe Firefly?

Adobe Firefly is the only AI image generator trained on Adobe Stock and public domain content, making it commercially safe. Integrated into Photoshop, Illustrator, and Express, it lets you generate, extend, and fill images non-destructively. For designers working with corporate clients, Firefly's legal safety is a major selling point.
