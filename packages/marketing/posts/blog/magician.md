# Magician Review: AI design tools inside Figma

## What is Magician?

Magician is a Figma plugin that brings AI directly into your design workflow. It can generate icons, copy, and images from text prompts without leaving your canvas. For designers who live in Figma, this removes the friction of switching between tools. The icon generation is particularly impressive for quickly mocking up UI elements.
