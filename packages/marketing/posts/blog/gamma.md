# Gamma Review: AI-powered presentations

## What is Gamma?

Gamma creates beautiful presentations, documents, and websites from a single text prompt. The AI handles layout, typography, and visual design so you can focus on content. The one-page scroll format is modern and engaging. Perfect for pitch decks, portfolios, and quick documentation that needs to look polished.

- **Text to deck**: Text to deck
- **One-page docs**: One-page docs
- **AI images**: AI images

## Cons

Gamma is ideal for presentation who need text to deck without the steep learning curve.
