# Stable Diffusion Review: Open-source AI image generation

## What is Stable Diffusion?

Stable Diffusion is the open-source AI image model that powers countless tools and workflows. Run it locally for unlimited, free generation, or use it through various platforms. SDXL and the newer SD3 models offer quality comparable to proprietary alternatives. For privacy-conscious designers and those who want full control, local Stable Diffusion is unbeatable.
